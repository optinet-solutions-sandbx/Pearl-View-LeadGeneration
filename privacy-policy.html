Privacy Policy

Overview

Pearl View ("we", "us", or "our") operates a window cleaning services business and uses WhatsApp Business messaging to communicate with clients and leads. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you interact with us through our website, contact forms, or WhatsApp messaging channel.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information from you:

  • Name — provided when you submit a contact form or call us
  • Phone number — to return calls, send quotes, and appointment confirmations
  • Email address — for quote follow-ups and service confirmations
  • Service address / property details — required to assess and deliver the cleaning service
  • Call recordings — inbound calls may be recorded for quality and follow-up purposes
  • WhatsApp messages — messages sent to or received from our WhatsApp Business account

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the purpose of providing our window cleaning services:

  • Responding to enquiries and providing quotes
  • Scheduling and confirming cleaning appointments
  • Sending reminders and service updates via WhatsApp
  • Processing payments and issuing invoices
  • Improving our service quality

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

WhatsApp Messaging

We use the WhatsApp Business Platform (provided by Meta Platforms, Inc.) to communicate with customers. When you message us on WhatsApp or opt in to receive messages from us:

  • Your WhatsApp phone number and messages are processed by Meta in accordance with WhatsApp's Privacy Policy.
  • We only send messages related to your service enquiry, appointment, or quote.
  • You may opt out of WhatsApp communications at any time by replying STOP or contacting us directly.

Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely in our internal CRM system (Airtable). We apply industry-standard security measures including access controls and encrypted connections. Only authorised Pearl View staff can access your data.

We retain your data for as long as necessary to fulfil the service or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Cookies

Our website may use basic cookies for analytics. No personal data is collected via cookies, and no third-party advertising trackers are used.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
